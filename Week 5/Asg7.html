Damon Salvatore

The impulsive older Salvatore brother who transitions from a Season 1 villain to a hero. Initially driven by a centuries-long obsession with Katherine Pierce, he eventually finds redemption through his deep, consuming love for Elena.

Elena Gilbert

The compassionate "doppelgänger" at the center of the series. She begins as a human caught in a love triangle with the Salvatore brothers, eventually becomes a vampire, and ultimately chooses a human life with Damon.

Klaus Mikhaelson

The "Original Hybrid" and one of the most powerful beings in existence. Introduced as a ruthless antagonist, he develops a soft spot for Caroline, famously promising to be her "last love". Wikipedia

Caroline Forbes

Elena’s insecure human best friend who flourishes after being turned into a vampire. She becomes the show's moral compass, eventually marrying Stefan Salvatore, and is known for her strong, organized personality.
