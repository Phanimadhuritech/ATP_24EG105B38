ORGINALS

Niklaus "Klaus" Mikaelson is a central figure in The Vampire Diaries (TVD) and the lead protagonist of its spin-off, The Originals. He is widely regarded as one of the most powerful and complex characters in the franchise.

Elijah Mikaelson is the moral compass and "noble" brother of the Original family. Known for his impeccably tailored suits and composed demeanor, he is a man of his word who values honor above all else.

Rebekah Mikaelson is the only female Original vampire. While she can be as fierce and lethal as her brothers, her character is defined by a deep, aching desire to be human and to be loved [1].

Kol Mikaelson is known as the "wildest" and most unpredictable of the Mikaelson siblings. While he starts as a violent antagonist, he undergoes significant growth, particularly through his romance with Davina Claire.
