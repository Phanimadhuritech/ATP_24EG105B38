Tropical Lagoon Grill

The restaurant

Tropical Lagoon Grill is located on a picturesque tropical island, renowned for its fresh seafood cuisine including daily-caught fish, shrimp, and lobster dishes prepared with local herbs and spices.

Caterng

The menu also features tropical fruit salads, grilled meats, and signature cocktails made with fresh juices

Location and hours

Tropical Lagoon Grill is located on the island of Bora Bora in French Polynesia, nestled along a pristine lagoon with direct access to crystal-clear waters. Operating hours are from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily, with extended hours during peak tourist seasons. The restaurant is closed on Mondays for maintenance. Reservations are recommended, especially for sunset dining
