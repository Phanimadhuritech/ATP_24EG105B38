The Vampire Diaries

The Vampire Diaries series explores the supernatural history of Mystic Falls, featuring an intense mix of romance, ancient curses, and legendary rivalries.

Damon Salvatore

The impulsive older Salvatore brother who transitions from a Season 1 villain to a hero. Initially driven by a centuries-long obsession with Katherine Pierce, he eventually finds redemption through his deep,consuming love for Elena.

Stefan Salvatore

Stefan Salvatore is a vampire doppelgänger from The Vampire Diaries known for his brooding nature and "Ripper" bloodlust. He is the noble younger brother of Damon Salvatore who eventually sacrifices his life to save his loved ones.

Elena Gilbert

The compassionate "doppelgänger" at the center of the series. She begins as a human caught in a love triangle with the Salvatore brothers, eventually becomes a vampire, and ultimately chooses a human life with Damon.

Jermey Gilbert

Jeremy Gilbert is Elena’s younger brother who evolves from a troubled teenager into a powerful supernatural Vampire Hunter. He possesses the ability to see ghosts and is often driven by a protective, resilient nature toward his friends.

Tyler Lockwood

"nitially Tyler was a typical high school jock and bully, his character arc becomes one of the most complex in the show as he navigates his identity as the first werewolf-vampire hybrid."

Caroline Forbes

Elena's insecure human best friend who flourishes after being turned into a vampire. She becomes the show's moral compass, eventually marrying Stefan Salvatore, and is known for her strong, organized personality.

Lorenzo

Lorenzo "Enzo" St. John is a charismatic vampire and former Augustine prisoner who becomes a devoted ally to the Salvatore group. He is best known for his deep, redemptive romance with Bonnie and his eventual sacrifice at the hands of Stefan.

Bonnie Bennett

Bonnie Bennett is a powerful witch and the moral heart of The Vampire Diaries, known for her immense selflessness. She frequently sacrifices her own happiness and life to save her friends and the town of Mystic Falls.

Matt Donovan

Matt Donovan is a is uniquely known for being the only main character to remain entirely human throughout all eight seasons of the show.

Alaric Saltzman

A history teacher and vampire hunter who becomes a father figure to the Gilbert siblings.
